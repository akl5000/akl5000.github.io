Spirit Beast

January 01, 0001
Races

Spirit Beast

The great Spirit Beasts of old once ruled the continent. Mystical dragons, serpents, fish, and even birds lived peacefully in the wilderness as they pleased. When conflict arose between the spirits of old and the conquering Humans, the most powerful Spirit Beasts took up arms to defeat the invaders. The two races met on the battlefield, with the elder spirit beasts arriving in humanoid form. The battle ended in stalemate, with both sides gaining a respect for each other, man to beast.

Human and Spirit Beast alliances are created, each benefiting equally. Some Spirits rise to become proud leaders of Human communities. Some venture into the world to prove their worth. Many of these become adventurers, achieving greatness for their mighty deeds.

Scarred and Strong

Spirit Beasts exhibit a blend of beast and human characteristics, and their appearance varies widely depending on the physical characteristics of their spirit. They are all at once indistinguishable from wild beasts yet as powerful as any human master.

Spirit Beasts regard battle scars as tokens of pride and ornamental scars as things of beauty. Other scars, though, mark a Spirit Beast as a prisoner of war, or an exiled criminal. Any Spirit Beast who has lived among or near orcs has scars, whether they are marks of humiliation or of pride, recounting their past exploits and injuries.

Protector of the Wilds

A spirit beast is born from the wild, into the wild, and serves the wild. While other races may find joy in conquering, power, and greed, a Spirit Beast’s goal is to protect its environment. The most successful Spirit beasts cause untouched terrain to persist through the centuries. Large amounts of Spirit beasts can gather in a populated area when a powerful natural object desires protection. Other spirit beasts are more than stationary. Distressed Spirits from other natural objects that flow through the wind can lead willing Spirit Beasts on adventures, fighting to protect its lesser brethren.

Spirit Beast Regions and Ethnicities

The spirit Beast occupy the southern regions of Eribar. Blocked by the Straight of Elgore on the west and the Agawrathe Mountains on the east, Spirit beasts are largely left to their own devices from the neighboring human settlements. No matter the Spirit of a Spirit Beast, Beasts of all kinds can be found in all regions of the Spirit Beast kingdom, but due to geological differences, many beasts have preferences upon which land they protect.

The Riven Jungle

The Riven Jungle Homes Monkey, Cat, and Elephant Spirits and is the home of the legendary White Tiger Spirit Baihu.

The Silver Marsh

The Silver Marsh is home to all nature of Snake, Turtle, Bear, and boar spirits, including the legendary hydra spirit Xiangliu.

Agawrathe Mountains

The Agawrathe Mountains is home to the legendary Phoenix Spirit Luan, as well as wyvern, goat, and bird Spirits.

Spirit Beast Traits

Your Spirit Beast Character has certain traits deriving from the Spirit Animal.

Ability Score Increase

Your Wisdom score increases by 1

Age

Spirit Beasts mature at the same rate as humans, but they’re considered young until they reach the age of 50. On average, they live about 350 years.

Size

Spirit Beasts Size Depends on what form they are in. All Spirit Beasts in human form vary between 5 to 6 feet tall and are Medium sized. The size in beasts form depends on the chosen animal spirit.

Speed

Your base walking speed is 30 feet.

Haven’t lost touch

You gain proficiency in the Animal Handling skill

Darkvision

Thanks to your animal blood, you have superior vision in dark and dim conditions when you shift. You can see in dim light within 60 feet of you as if it were bright light, and in darkness as if it were dim light. You can’t discern color in darkness, only shades of gray.

Shifting

As a bonus action, you can assume the form of your Beast Spirit. This transformation lasts for 3 minutes, until you die, or until you revert to your normal appearance as a bonus action. When you shift, you gain temporary hit points equal to your level + your Constitution modifier (minimum of 1 temporary hit point). You also gain benefits that depend on your Spirit Beast subrace, described below. Once you shift, you can’t do so again until you finish a short or long rest.

Cat

As a cat-based Spirit Beast, you have keen senses, quick actions, and an agile body. The Cat Spirits are renowned in the Spirit beast kingdom to be the best fighters and the most able bodied to learn a sword.

Ability Score Increase

Your Dexterity score increases by 2

Size

Size in Cat form depends on the species of cat chosen. Regardless of species, the official Size in this form is medium.

Keen Senses

You have proficiency in the Perception skill.

Shifting Feature: Quickness

While shifted, your walking speed increases by an additional 5 feet. Additionally, you can move up to 10 feet as a reaction when an enemy ends its turn within 5 feet of you. This movement doesn’t provoke opportunity attacks.

Turtle

As a Turtle-based Spirit Beast, you have great toughness, surprising power, and concentrated spirit. Turtle Spirits are known to be some of the greatest protectors of all the Spirit Beasts.

Ability Score Increase

Your Constitution score increases by 2

Size

Size in this form is medium.

Hard body

You have proficiency in the Athletics skill

Shifting Feature: Hardness

Whenever you shift, you gain (1d6 + your constitution modifier) additional temporary hit points, a +1 bonus to your AC, and your base walking speed is set to 25.

Snake

As a Snake-based Spirit Beast, you have elusive movement and a small stature. This type of Spirit beast is renowned for information gathering and decisiveness.

Ability Score Increase

Your Intelligence score increases by 1

Size

Size in this form is tiny.

Hidden in plain sight

You have proficiency in the investigation skill

Shifting Feature: Elusiveness

While shifted, if a dodge action is chosen as your action for the turn, you may choose one additional action to perform at the same time. Your base walking speed in this form is 30 feet.

Bird

As a bird-based Spirit beast, you protect the skies of the Spirit beast territory. Children look up to you in awe and cherish your protection. Many great strategists and generals have been Bird Spirits during the golden era of the kingdom.

Ability Score Increase

Your Wisdom and Charisma score increases by 1

Size

Size in this form is small.

Keen Senses

You have proficiency in the Perception skill.

Shifting Feature: Flight

While shifted, you have a flying speed of 50 feet. All unarmed attacks in this form deal an additional 1d4 slashing damage on hit. All ranged attacks made while in the air have disadvantage.
