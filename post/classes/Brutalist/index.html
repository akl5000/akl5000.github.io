January 01, 0001
Classes

Brutalist

While other fighting styles choose to imbue their weaponry and movements with essence. Brutalists take a more intrusive approach. By brutally infusing their bodies with essence, an unarmed fighting style becomes most effective. Whether or not a brutalist uses this power to bring health or destruction is up to them.

Imbued palms

The brutalist fighting style utilizes the lightweight capabilities of martial arts. With the essence flowing through their bodies, their palms become as deadly as a sword. Brutalists harness this power to create magical effects and exceed their bodies physical capabilities. Some brutalists study the essence that flows through their bodies, opting to help mend the wounds of others around them, while other choose to display the destructive power they can output with every hand. As they gain experience, their martial training and their mastery of ki gives them more power over their bodies and the bodies of their foes.

Isolation

Most Brutalists choose to live an isolated life. The process of imbuing your blood is a brutal one, often taking many years of rigorous, painful training to become one. Some Brutalists live entirely apart from the surrounding population, secluded from anything that might impede their training. Others are sworn to isolation, emerging only to serve as great forces on the battlefield, at the command of their leader, a noble patron, or sometimes the scale itself.

Quick Build

You can make a monk quickly by following these suggestions. First, make Strength your highest ability score, followed by Charisma. You may value Charisma more than Strength if you value support over damage. Second, choose the hermit background.

Class Features

As a Brutalist, you gain the following class features

Hit Points

  • Hit Dice: 1d8 per Brutalist level
  • Hit Points at 1st Level: 8 + your Constitution modifier
  • Hit Points at Higher Levels: 1d8 (or 5) + your Constitution modifier per Brutalist level after 1st

Proficiencies

  • Armor: Light armor
  • Weapons: None
  • Tools: Choose one type of artisan tools
  • Saving Throws: Strength, Wisdom
  • Skills: Choose two from Acrobatics, Athletics, Religion, Sleight of hand, Deception, Animal Handling

Equipment

You start with the following equipment, in addition to the equipment granted by your background:

  • (a) Padded armor or*(b)* Leather armor
  • (a) a dungeoneer’s pack or (b) an explorer’s pack

Martial Arts

At 1st level, your practice of martial arts gives you mastery of combat styles that use palm strikes. You gain the following benefits while you are unarmed.

You can use Charisma instead of Strength for the attack and damage rolls of your palm strikes.

You can roll a d4 in place of the normal damage of your palm strike. This die changes as you gain Brutalist levels, as shown in the Palm strikes column of the class table.

When you use the Attack action with a palm strike on your turn, you can make an additional palm strike as a bonus action. For example, if you take the Attack action and attack with your palm, you can also make a palm strike as a bonus action, assuming you haven’t already taken a bonus action this turn.

Essence assistance

Starting at 2nd level, your training allows you to harness the lingering essence of the scales. Your access to this energy is represented by a number of essence points. Your Brutalist level determines the number of points you have, as shown in the essence column of the Brutalist table.

You can spend these points to fuel various essence features. You start knowing three such features: Flurry of Blows, Patient Defense, and Step of the Wind. You learn more essence features as you gain levels in this class.

When you spend an essence point, it is unavailable until you finish a short or long rest, at the end of which you draw all of your expended essence back into yourself.

Some of your essence features require your target to make a saving throw to resist the feature’s effects. The saving throw DC is calculated as follows:

Essence save DC = 8 + your proficiency bonus + your Strength modifier

  • Flurry of Blows
    Immediately after you take the Attack action on your turn, you can spend 1 essence point to make two unarmed strikes as a bonus action.

  • Patient Defense
    You can spend 1 essence point to take the Dodge action as a bonus action on your turn.

  • Step of the Wind
    You can spend 1 essence point to take the Disengage or Dash action as a bonus action on your turn, and your jump distance is doubled for the turn.

Unhanded Dexterity

Beginning at 3rd level, you can use your reaction when you fall to reduce any falling damage you take by an amount equal to five times your monk level.

Essence divergence

Starting at 3rd level, the essence swirling inside you becomes chaotic. You must make a choice between what energy to accept within yourself. Your choice adds features again at 6th, 9th, and 12th levels. The energies you may choose between are listed at the bottom of the class features.

Ability Score Improvement

When you reach 4th level, and again at 8th, 11th, 16th, and 19th level, you can increase one ability score of your choice by 2, or you can increase two ability scores of your choice by 1. As normal, you can’t increase an ability score above 20 using this feature.

Open Hand Technique

At 5th level, you can manipulate your enemy’s essence when you harness your own. Whenever you hit a creature with one of the attacks granted by a bonus attack, for 1 essence point, you can impose one of the following effects on that target:

  • It must succeed on a Dexterity saving throw or be knocked prone.

  • It must make a Strength saving throw. If it fails, you can push it up to 15 feet away from you.

  • It can’t take reactions until the end of your next turn.

Infused blood

At 7th level, you gain the ability to heal yourself. As an action, you can regain hit points equal to three times your Brutalist level. You must finish a long rest before you can use this feature again.

Pure Blood

At 10th level, your mastery of the essence flowing through you makes you immune to disease and poison.

Essence Fueled

Beginning at 13th level, your mastery of essence grants you proficiency in all saving throws. Additionally, whenever you make a saving throw and fail, you can spend 1 essence point to reroll it and take the second result.

Unhanded Movement

At 14th level, your speed while having no weapon unsheathed increases by 30 feet.

Compromising Essence

At 15th level, your essence has matured enough to allow other forms of energy to coexist within your system. You gain the features of the other Essence choice. Its features granted at 3rd level are granted at 15th level. Its features granted at 6th level are granted at 17th level. Its features granted at 9th level are granted at 18th level. Its features granted at 12th level are granted at 20th level.

Essence Choices

Essence swirls inside your body, clashing against each other. The use of your attacks with the aid of uncontrolled essence has made your foundation unstable, and you must take some losses to survive. The two essences that swirl around your body are wood and control. Choose one of the options listed below. Your choice grants you features at 3rd, 6th 9th and 12th levels.

Forgo control

When you drain the control essence writhing within you, you can feel the wood essence relax inside your blood. With its newfound space, you feel your strength will grow.

At 3rd level, you gain proficiency in the (a) Medicine skill or (b) History skill

Hands of Healing

At 3rd level, your mystical touch can mend wounds. As an action, you can spend 1 essence point to touch a target and restore a number of hit points equal to a roll of your Palm die + your Charisma modifier. When you use your Flurry of Blows, you can replace one of the Palm strikes with a use of this feature without spending an essence point for the healing.

Imbue wood

Starting at 6th level, you can administer even greater cures with a touch, and if you feel it’s necessary, you can use your knowledge to cause harm.

When you use Hands of Healing on a target, you can also end one disease or one of the following conditions affecting the target: blinded, deafened, paralyzed, poisoned, or stunned.

Also at 6th level, when you use Hands of Healing on a target, you may instead attack with an unarmed strike that inflicts to the poisoned condition on hit until the end of your next turn. This can only be used once per turn.

Flurry of Healing and Harm

Starting at 9th level, you can now output a flurry of comfort and hurt. When you use Flurry of Blows, you can now replace each of the palm strikes with a use of your Hands of Healing, without spending essence points for the healing.

Woodland Terrain

By 12th level, as a bonus action, you can create an aura of the scale’s essence that radiates 15 feet from you for 1 minute. For the duration choose a damage type when you activate this aura: acid, cold, fire, lightning, or poison. You and your allies within the aura have resistance to that damage.

Forgo Wood

As you feel the wood essence leave your blood, you feel something else fill up the negative space: rage essence. You panic for a moment but feel relaxed after realizing you instead feel internally balanced.

At 3rd level, you gain proficiency in the (a) Intimidation or (b) Perception skill.

The rage essence and the control essence contradict and balance themselves inside your blood. You gain a number of spells and attacks that utilizes the rage and control essence inside you. You may use these abilities as a bonus action once per turn. The first essence attack of a combat can be a rage or control ability. All turns after that, the abilities must alternate between the two essences, even after a turn without an attack.

Basic Spellcasting

At 3rd level, you gain access to all rage and control cantrips. These cantrips can be used as an action once a turn.

Palms of Pride and Grace.

At 6th level, you gain access to two Essence attacks. You may use these attacks as an action by spending 1 essence point.

Palm of Pride

  • Essence type: Rage
  • Damage: 3d6 fire damage
  • Effects: As you hold your hands with thumbs touching and fingers spread, a thin sheet of flames shoots forth from your outstretched fingertips. Each creature in a 15-foot cone must make a Dexterity saving throw. A creature takes 3d6 fire damage on a failed save, or half as much damage on a successful one.

Palm of Grace

  • Essence type: Control
  • Damage: 2d8
  • Effects: A wave of grace sweeps out from you. Each creature of your choosing in a 15-foot cube originating from you must make a Constitution saving throw. On a failed save, a creature takes 2d8 force damage and is pushed 10 feet away from you. On a successful save, the creature takes half as much damage and isn’t pushed.

Essence explosion

Starting at 9th level, as an action, you may let out your essence in a large and fast explosion from your body for 3 essence points. This ability has different effects based on if you use it as a control- or rage-based ability.

-Control - Each creature of your choosing in a 30 ft radius must succeed on a constitution saving throw or take 5d6 force damage

-Rage - Each creature of in a 20 ft radius must succeed on a constitution saving throw or take 4d12 force damage.

Essence of War and peace.

At 12th level, you gain access to two more Essence attacks. You may use these attacks as an action by spending 4 essence points. You may only have one of these abilities active at a given time.

Essence of War

  • Essence type: Rage
  • Damage: 3d10 Force damage repeatable
  • Effects: You create a sword-shaped plane of force that hovers within range for 1 minute. It lasts for the duration. When the sword appears, you make a melee attack against a target of your choice within 5 feet of the sword. On a hit, the target takes 3d10 force damage. Until the spell ends, you can use a bonus action on each of your turns to move the sword up to 20 feet to a spot you can see and repeat this attack against the same target or a different one.

Essence of Peace

  • Essence type: Control
  • Damage: N/A
  • *Effects:*You send the essence imbued inside you to a target of your choosing. Until the spell ends, you and the target have resistance to bludgeoning, piercing, and slashing damage.
