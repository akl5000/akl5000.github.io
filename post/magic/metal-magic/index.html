Metal Magic

Rules of spells. These spells are taken 1 for 1 from DND. A link to the DND spell will be included. The At higher levels mechanic is ignored, the Components mechanic is ignored, and the Level mechanic is ignored. The essence cost will always be listed. Cantrips are listed first, followed by all the other spells.

Cantrips are simple spells that you may use with only a little understanding of essence. The classes that gain access to cantrips are Swordsman, Beggar, Brutalist, and Soundsmith. Cantrips are divided into the same categories as the other spells. The only exception is that they cost no essence. The only requirement is if you have imbued yourself with the corresponding scales’ essence.
