Cantrips are listed first, followed by all the other spells.

Cantrips are simple spells that you may use with only a little understanding of essence. The classes that gain access to cantrips are Swordsman, Beggar, Brutalist, and Soundsmith. Cantrips are divided into the same categories as the other spells. The only exception is that they cost no essence.
